About Me

My name is Daniel, and I majored at UWT with a bachelors in social work. It seems to be oddly placed that I wanted to start studying programming when my major is so far different from it, but there are reasons why I decided to enroll. I always enjoyed creating things and using computers ever since I was a little kid. I feel like I have a mind for solving problems, just like I try doing in my job. I always enjoyed the paperwork side of my job better anyway. Aside from this, I am also just tired of being broke - because the social work field does not pay much or offer benefits that I would ever want to be a breadwinner with. After elevating my career to something that utilizes computer skills in it, the next problem I will run into is how to stop being single. But that's not some thing I need to get into right now.
